COOKIES


From our insanely decadent "Chocolate Rads" to almond macaroon and molasses ginger, our cookies can be found at school picnics, winery events, office parties, and home kitchens throughout the Valley. But the best place is in your hands, with a cold glass of milk or one of our Stumptown coffee concoctions. In addition to our daily lineup of these tasty treats, we love to bake our seasonal favorites - cookies for Valentine's Day, Easter, Halloween, Thanksgiving, and of course... mom's famous iced Christmas Cookies!


Visit the Napa location in the afternoons and you may be so lucky to receive a warm baguette during our "late bake."















Beware... our cookies are addictive! Baked fresh every morning in our historic brick ovens, our cookies have been a Napa Valley favorite for decades.
